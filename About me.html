About Me

Bio

I am a frontend developer who is passionate about transforming creative ideas into interactive, accessible, and visually appealing web experiences. I enjoy working at the intersection of design and technology where user experience meets clean, maintainable code. My journey into frontend development began with simple curiosity: how do websites come to life?
Over time, that curiosity grew into a passion for learning modern web technologies like CSS, and JavaScript. I love the satisfaction of seeing a layout come together or debugging a tricky issue until everything works perfectly.
Outside of coding, I am interested in UI/UX design, and I enjoy exploring tools like Figma to better understand how good design decisions translate into better user experiences.

Goals in this program

  • Strengthen my foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript by following best practices and writing semantic, accessible code.
  • Improve my understanding of frontend frameworks like React, including component structure and state management.
  • Collaborate effectively with other developers, practice good Git/GitHub workflows, and write clear commit messages.
  • Build confidence in presenting my work and explaining my technical decisions clearly to both developers and non-technical audiences.

Areas of low confidence

I am still working on building confidence in debugging complex JavaScript logic and managing state efficiently in React applications.
Sometimes, I also find it challenging to structure CSS for large projects in a scalable and maintainable way.

Note to future self

Keep pushing forward, even when things get tough. Remember how far I have come from learning my first “Hello World” to building functional web applications.
Continue exploring new frameworks, stay curious, and never stop improving.

Extra thoughts
