About Me

Bio

I am a frontend developer passionate about transforming creative ideas into interactive, accessible, and visually appealing web experiences. I enjoy working at the intersection of design and technology, where user experience meets clean, maintainable code.

My journey into frontend development began with curiosity—how do websites come to life? Over time, that curiosity evolved into a deep passion for learning modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I love the satisfaction of seeing a layout come together or debugging a tricky issue until everything works perfectly.

Outside of coding, I am interested in UI/UX design and love exploring tools like Figma to understand how good design decisions translate into great user experiences.

Goals in This Program

  • Strengthen my foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript by following best practices and writing semantic, accessible code.
  • Improve my understanding of frontend frameworks like React, including component structure and state management.
  • Collaborate effectively with other developers, practice good Git/GitHub workflows, and write clear commit messages.
  • Build confidence in presenting my work and explaining my technical decisions clearly to both developers and non-technical audiences.

Areas of Low Confidence

I am still working on building confidence in debugging complex JavaScript logic and managing state efficiently in React applications. I also find it challenging at times to structure CSS for large projects in a scalable and maintainable way.

Note to Future Self

Keep pushing forward, even when things get tough. Remember how far you’ve come—from writing your first “Hello World” to building full-fledged web applications. Continue exploring new frameworks, stay curious, and never stop learning or improving.

Extra Thoughts

Being a great developer isn’t just about writing code—it’s about understanding people, solving problems, and creating experiences that make users’ lives easier. I’m excited to keep growing, learning, and contributing to meaningful projects that have real impact.
